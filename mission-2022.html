2022 Mission ow.

As yet another year dawned upon us all, peace was assumed. The International Gathering of people’s had disbanded as with the signing of the treaty which was due to shut down all organisations related to it, there was no longer a need for it. But as we reconnected with the world we found that The Blessed Empire had not abided by it’s side of the treaty, subsequent of that all plans to disband have been halted under Article Two, Section Three, as I with the majority delegated to I, fulfill a law regarding the sovereignty of the USSY. This new law reads as such:

Any treaty the USSY signs can be removed from law if any single other contractually bound party breaks, violates, or dishonours that treaty.

By this new law, the USSY can and has abandoned the treaty of shutting down, this law will later be put up for vote on whether it should be incorporated into the constitution or left as a law which can be overwritten at any time.



Due to this unprecedented violation, the International Gathering of People’s has reassembled under the rule of Matthew Rowe, & the USSY has begun processing as per normal. To continue to normality which we near gave up, this document has been written to establish the goals and means to complete said goals, which the USSY shall complete this new year.
